FAQ Page

  • Q1

    Do you guys write your own recipes?

    We don't generally write the recipes on our site. They are sourced from a diverse selection of websites, magazines, and books. We may edit and change the wording here and there, but for the most part, we try to keep the recipes as they are written in the orginal source. If you're curious about the orginal text, the author and source are always sited on each recipe page.

  • Q2

    Could I suggest a recipe for the site?

    Absolutely! On our Contact Us Page you can suggest recipes you would like to see on the site. In fact, we even encourage readers to write their own recipes and share them if they feel so inclined. We want to create a community here and would love to hear from you!

  • Q3

    Did you make your own website?

    Both the CSS and HTML for this website was written from scratch by us!
