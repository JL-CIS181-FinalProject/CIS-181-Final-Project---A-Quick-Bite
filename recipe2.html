Cheesy Baked Broccoli


Recipe by Lauren Miyashiro at delish.com


Despite how simple it seems, this dish is very versatile. It can be served as a side, snack, or meal and there is usually enough to share with friends or family. A lot can be done to personalize the dish as well, so feel free to add your own ideas such as cheese you like, or additions such as cauliflower or shrimp!


Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 15 mins Total Time: 30 min Servings: 4-6

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 large head of broccoli, cut into florets
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ c heavy cream
  • ½ c shredded mozzarella
  • ½ c shredded Gruyere
  • ¼ c grated Parmesan
  • Red pepper flakes
  • Chopped parsley (garnish)
  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees. In a large oven-proof skillet over medium heat, melt butter with olive oil. Add broccoli and season it with salt and pepper. Cook until bright green and crisp-tender (~5 minutes). Stir in garlic and cook until fragrant (~1 minute).
  2. Pour heavy cream over the broccoli then top with mozzarella, Gruyere, Parmesan, and red pepper flakes. Bake until cheese is bubbly and broccoli is tender (~10 minutes). If your cheese won’t brown, try heating it under the broiler for about a minute.
  3. Serve immediately.
