Creamy Pesto Penne with Sausage


Recipe by BaughHumbug at allrecipes.com


This pasta dish is perfect for a nice dinner shared with a friend. Despite only requiring five different ingredients, the dish has a strong and delicious flavor. With only three steps that take about thirty minutes, this creamy pesto penne is a perfect pick for a quick but fulfilling dinner.


Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 25 min Servings: 2

Ingredients:

  • 1 (16 oz) package of penne pasta
  • ½ lb ground sausage
  • 1 (1.2 oz) package of creamy pesto sauce mix
  • 1 c milk
  • ¼ c olive oil
  1. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook the penne at a boil, stirring occasionally, until the pasta is tender yet firm to the bite (about 11 minutes). Drain and transfer the pasta to a bowl.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir the ground sausage in the hot skillet until browned and crumbly (5 to 7 minutes). Add the sausage to the pasta.
  3. Whisk the pesto sauce mix, milk, and olive oil together in a small saucepan. Bring the mix to a boil while stirring constantly. Reduce heat to low and keep at a simmer until thickened (about 3 minutes). Pour the sauce over your pasta and sausage mixture then toss to coat.
